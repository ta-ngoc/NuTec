Hypertension (high blood pressure)
Nearly 60% of older adults were treated for hypertension. When your heart pumps a lot of blood, and you have narrow arteries which resist the flow, that`s when you get high blood pressure, also known as hypertension. The danger of hypertension is not only that you can have it for years and not know it, but it can cause other serious health conditions, like stroke and heart attacks.
